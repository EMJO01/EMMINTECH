My journey

How I got here

The Beginning

Curiosity meets the internet

Like most developers, it started with curiosity — inspecting web pages, tweaking HTML, wondering how the whole thing worked. I started teaching myself to code, one YouTube video and one broken div at a time.

First Projects

Built my first digital contact card

My first real project — a sleek personal contact card to consolidate my online presence. It wasn't perfect, but it worked. More importantly, it showed me what was possible with just HTML and CSS.

First Client

Starr Fruitti — my first paid project

Built a full landing page for a fruit store client. Seeing a real business go live with something I built from scratch was the moment everything clicked. I knew this was more than a hobby.

Founding Nexua

Turning skills into a company

I launched Nexua to formalise what I was already doing — building digital products for businesses. Nexua is the vehicle for everything: web development, design, hosting, and future tech innovations.

Now

Growing, building, shipping

6 projects live. More in the pipeline. Every project teaches me something new and brings me closer to my vision — a future where great digital work is accessible to every business, not just the ones with big budgets.
