Pricing Rules

Never Discount
The price is the price. If flexibility is needed, add value to the package — never lower the number.
50% Upfront. Always.
"I start work once the deposit is in — that's how I keep delivery timelines tight."
State Price Once
After quoting, go silent. Filling silence with justification signals you don't believe in your own value.
Bundle to Upsell
If someone balks at price, reframe: "The bundle gives you X, Y, and Z for less than buying each separately."
Retainers at Delivery
Always pitch maintenance/retainer at delivery. Easier to say yes when the project just impressed them.
Discovery Call Over $1,500
Don't quote a custom project without a call first. Underquoting kills premium positioning.
